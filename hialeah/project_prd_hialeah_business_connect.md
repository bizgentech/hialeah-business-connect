# Project PRD: Hialeah Business Connect
**Status:** MVP Design Phase Complete  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** BizGen Technologies  

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Hialeah Business Connect is a civic-tech discovery ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between local small businesses, residents, and visitors in Hialeah, FL. Unlike generic directories, this platform integrates web discovery, physical interactive kiosks, and a robust admin layer to drive commercial visibility and economic growth.

## 2. Strategic Objectives
- **Local Visibility:** Provide a free, high-quality digital presence for every Hialeah business.
- **Economic Engagement:** Facilitate discovery through map-based search and hyper-local offer promotions.
- **Phygital Integration:** Connect physical city locations (kiosks) to the digital platform via "Send to Phone" QR workflows.
- **Sustainable Growth:** Create monetization pathways through featured placements and kiosk ad inventory for BizGen Technologies.

## 3. Target Audience
1. **Residents & Visitors:** Users looking for trusted local services, restaurants, and deals.
2. **Business Owners:** Entrepreneurs seeking visibility without technical overhead.
3. **Admin/Partners:** BizGen Technologies and city-affiliated organizers managing the ecosystem.

## 4. Feature Roadmap (MVP)

### 4.1. Public Web Platform
- **Intelligent Directory:** Category-filtered search with integrated map view.
- **Rich Business Profiles:** High-fidelity listings featuring galleries, contact CTAs, and verification badges.
- **Community Offers:** A dedicated hub for local coupons and promotions.
- **Self-Service Onboarding:** Low-friction submission form for new business listings.

### 4.2. Interactive Kiosk System
- **Touch-First UI:** Optimized for high-traffic physical discovery stations.
- **Nearby Exploration:** Map-centric view highlighting businesses within walking distance.
- **Mobile Handover:** QR code integration to transfer business data to mobile devices instantly.

### 4.3. Admin Management Suite
- **Verification Workflow:** Queue-based system for approving or rejecting new submissions.
- **Monetization Tools:** Management of featured placements and kiosk-specific ad campaigns.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** High-level tracking of total businesses, active offers, and kiosk engagement.

## 5. Design System & Visual Identity

## 6. Technical Specifications
- **Frontend:** Next.js / React / Tailwind CSS
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **Asset Management:** Cloud-native storage for business imagery and logos.
- **Kiosk Target:** Android-based interactive digital signage browsers.
